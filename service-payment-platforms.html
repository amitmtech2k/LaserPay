Payment Platform Development

Payment Platform Development is ZentiqOne's engineering practice for building the core systems that let a business process, route, and settle digital payments — including payment gateways, aggregation platforms, wallets, and collection or disbursement systems. It is built for banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, payment aggregators, and enterprises that need to launch or scale a payments product rather than assemble one from generic software components.

What's Included

  • Payment Gateway Development — engineering the transaction processing core that authorizes, routes, and settles card, UPI, and net-banking payments.
  • Payment Aggregator Solutions — building the technology stack a Payment Aggregator needs to onboard and settle merchants at scale.
  • Payment Orchestration — routing transactions across multiple acquiring banks and gateways to improve success rates and reduce cost.
  • Merchant Platforms — merchant-facing dashboards, reporting, and settlement views layered on top of the payment stack.
  • Wallet Platforms — closed and semi-closed wallet systems with load, transfer, and redemption flows.
  • UPI Solutions — UPI collect, pay, and intent flows integrated with NPCI and partner banks.
  • BBPS — Bharat Bill Payment System integrations for bill aggregation and payment.
  • Escrow — escrow account structures and fund-flow logic for marketplace and aggregator models.
  • Collections — automated collection flows for recurring and one-time payments.
  • Disbursements — bulk and API-driven payout systems for vendor, salary, and loan disbursements.

Who This Is For

This service is built for banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, payment aggregators, marketplaces, and enterprises that are building or scaling a payments product and need engineering depth in transaction processing, routing, and settlement.
