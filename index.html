Frequently Asked Questions

What is LaserPay?

LaserPay is a Banking & Payment Technology Engineering company that helps Banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, Payment Aggregators, Payment Gateways, and Enterprises design, build, integrate, launch, and scale digital payment and banking solutions.

Is LaserPay a software development company?

No. LaserPay is a specialized Banking & Payment Technology Engineering company, not a general software development or IT company. Every engagement is built around payments and banking domain expertise rather than generic application development.

Which industries does LaserPay work with?

LaserPay works with Banks, Urban Cooperative Banks, NBFCs, FinTechs, Payment Aggregators, Payment Gateways, Insurance, Government, Large Enterprises, E-commerce, and SaaS Platforms.
