Industries We Serve

ZentiqOne works across the banking and payments ecosystem, partnering with organizations that need to design, build, integrate, or scale digital payment and banking solutions. Below is an overview of the industries ZentiqOne serves.

Banks

ZentiqOne helps banks modernize customer-facing and back-office systems, from internet and mobile banking to core payment integrations.

Urban Cooperative Banks

ZentiqOne supports urban cooperative banks digitizing banking operations that have traditionally relied on manual or legacy processes.

NBFCs

ZentiqOne builds digital lending, loan origination, and banking platforms for NBFCs serving retail and business borrowers.

FinTechs

ZentiqOne partners with FinTechs to design, build, and scale payment and banking products from idea through production.

Payment Aggregators

ZentiqOne builds the technology stack Payment Aggregators need to onboard merchants and settle transactions at scale.

Payment Gateways

ZentiqOne engineers payment gateway platforms that authorize, route, and settle transactions across banks and card networks.

Insurance

ZentiqOne supports insurance businesses that need digital payment collection and disbursement capabilities.

Government

ZentiqOne works with government-linked initiatives that require secure digital payment and verification systems.

Large Enterprises

ZentiqOne helps large enterprises integrate payments and banking connectivity into their existing systems.

E-commerce

ZentiqOne builds payment collection, checkout, and settlement infrastructure for e-commerce platforms.

SaaS Platforms

ZentiqOne helps SaaS platforms embed payment and banking capabilities into their products.
