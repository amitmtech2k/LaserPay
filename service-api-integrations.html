API Integrations

API Integrations is LaserPay's engineering practice for connecting a platform to the banking, card network, and regulatory systems it depends on, including banks, NPCI, Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, CBS, AML, CKYC, DigiLocker, Aadhaar, PAN, GST, account validation, NACH, UPI, BBPS, FASTag, and AEPS. It is built for any platform that needs reliable, production-grade connectivity to banking, card network, or regulatory and verification systems, rather than building and maintaining every integration in-house.

What's Included

  • Banks — core banking and payment integrations with partner and acquiring banks.
  • NPCI — integrations with National Payments Corporation of India switches and networks.
  • Visa — card network integrations for authorization, clearing, and settlement.
  • Mastercard — card network integrations for authorization, clearing, and settlement.
  • RuPay — domestic card network integrations for authorization, clearing, and settlement.
  • Payment Gateways — connectivity to third-party payment gateways and processors.
  • CBS — integration with Core Banking Systems for account and transaction data.
  • AML — integration with anti-money laundering screening and monitoring systems.
  • CKYC — integration with the Central KYC Registry for identity verification.
  • DigiLocker — integration for digital document retrieval and verification.
  • Aadhaar — integration for Aadhaar-based identity verification.
  • PAN — integration for PAN verification.
  • GST — integration for GST identification and verification.
  • Account Validation — bank account and penny-drop verification integrations.
  • NACH — National Automated Clearing House integrations for recurring debits and credits.
  • UPI — Unified Payments Interface integrations for collect, pay, and intent flows.
  • BBPS — Bharat Bill Payment System integrations for bill aggregation and payment.
  • FASTag — integrations for electronic toll collection systems.
  • AEPS — Aadhaar Enabled Payment System integrations for micro-ATM transactions.

Who This Is For

This service is built for banks, FinTechs, payment aggregators, payment gateways, and enterprises that need dependable connectivity to banking, card network, or regulatory and verification systems, whether for a single integration or a full integration layer.
