Merchant Enablement

Merchant Enablement is ZentiqOne's hands-on practice for onboarding merchants onto a payment platform, covering payment gateway selection, bank introductions, compliance, documentation, technical integration, and certification and go-live support. It is built for payment aggregators, payment gateways, and platforms that need to onboard merchants quickly and reliably at scale.

What's Included

  • Merchant Onboarding — structured onboarding workflows that move merchants from signup to live acceptance.
  • Payment Gateway Selection — guidance on choosing the payment gateway or aggregator that fits a merchant's business model.
  • Bank Introductions — introductions to acquiring banks and partner institutions for merchant settlement.
  • Compliance — support aligning merchant onboarding with regulatory and network compliance requirements.
  • Documentation — preparation and management of the documentation merchants need to go live.
  • Technical Integration — engineering support for merchant-side payment integration.
  • Go-Live Support — hands-on support through launch to resolve issues quickly.
  • Certification — support through card network and gateway certification processes.

Who This Is For

This service is built for payment aggregators, payment gateways, and platforms that need to onboard merchants quickly and reliably, from first contact through certified go-live.
