Banking Solutions

Banking Solutions is ZentiqOne's engineering practice for building the digital banking platforms banks, NBFCs, and urban cooperative banks use to serve retail and corporate customers, including internet banking, mobile banking, digital onboarding, video KYC, corporate banking, digital lending, and loan origination systems. It is built for banks, NBFCs, and urban cooperative banks digitizing customer-facing and back-office banking operations, rather than assembling banking capability from disconnected point solutions.

What's Included

  • Internet Banking — secure web-based banking portals for retail and business customers to manage accounts, payments, and services online.
  • Mobile Banking — native and hybrid mobile banking apps covering account access, transfers, and everyday banking needs.
  • Merchant Banking — banking interfaces and tools tailored to the merchant customers of a bank or NBFC.
  • Digital Onboarding — paperless account opening flows that reduce time-to-activation for new customers.
  • Video KYC — remote identity verification workflows aligned to regulatory KYC requirements.
  • Corporate Banking — multi-user, multi-approval banking platforms for business and corporate customers.
  • Digital Lending — end-to-end digital loan application, underwriting, and disbursement journeys.
  • Loan Origination Systems — systems that manage the loan lifecycle from application through approval and disbursement.

Who This Is For

This service is built for banks, NBFCs, and urban cooperative banks that are digitizing customer-facing and back-office banking operations and need a technology partner across internet banking, mobile banking, digital onboarding, corporate banking, and digital lending.
