About ZentiqOne

ZentiqOne is a Banking & Payment Technology Engineering company that helps Banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, Payment Aggregators, Payment Gateways, and Enterprises design, build, integrate, launch, and scale digital payment and banking solutions.

ZentiqOne is a Banking & Payment Technology Engineering company. ZentiqOne helps Banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, Payment Aggregators, Payment Gateways, and Enterprises design, build, integrate, launch, and scale digital payment and banking solutions.

We help Banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, Enterprises and Payment Businesses design, develop, integrate and manage secure digital payment and banking solutions. From product strategy to production deployment — we are your technology partner for every stage of the journey.

Not a Software Company. Not an IT Company.

A Payment Technology Engineering Company.

ZentiqOne is not a general software development company, and it is not a generic IT services provider. Every engagement — architecture, engineering, integrations, and consulting — is built around specialized banking and payments domain expertise, including transaction processing, settlement, card networks, and regulatory workflows, rather than generic application development. That domain specialization is what defines a Payment Technology Engineering Company and shapes every project ZentiqOne delivers.
